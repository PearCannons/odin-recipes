Portobello Steak Fajitas

Description

Fajitas are a wonderfully warm and comforting food that also provides lots of flavor. This vegan version gives us the meatiness that we crave and keeps the bell peppers and spices intact. If you make other vegan recipes, the various oil choices and toppings given here will provide flavor variants that are sure to make your lunch or dinner memorable.

The cashew cream option is a deliciously vegan way to get ones cream cheese fix. For those hoping to low carb, subsitute the flour tortillas for corn for a less fluffy yet no less delicious experience.

Ingredients

For the portobello steaks:

  • 4 to 6 large portobello mushrooms (about 1-1.25 lbs/450-565g)
  • 2 Tbsp + 1.5 tsp (37ml) grapeseed oil
  • 1 tsp (5ml) dried oregano
  • 1 tsp (5ml) ground cumin
  • .75 tsp (4ml) chili powder
  • .5 tsp (2ml) fine grain sea salt
  • freshly ground black pepper

For the stir fry:

  • 1 Tbsp (15 ml) grapeseed, olive or coconut oil
  • 1 large bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 large orange bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium yellow onion, thinly sliced

To assemble:

  • 4 to 6 small flour tortillas or lettuce leaves, for wrapping
  • sliced avocado
  • Cashew Cream
  • salsa
  • fresh lime juice
  • hot sauce
  • cilantro
  • shredded lettuce
